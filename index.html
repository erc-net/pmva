About PMVA

PMVA is a structured framework designed for healthcare, mental health, education, social care and security environments. It prioritises prevention, communication and safe intervention, using physical techniques only when absolutely necessary.

Recognise

Identify early warning signs and behavioural escalation.

Prevent

Use communication and de-escalation strategies effectively.

Protect

Safeguard staff, patients and vulnerable individuals.

Respond

Apply lawful and proportionate interventions when required.
